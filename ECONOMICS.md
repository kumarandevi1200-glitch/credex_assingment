# SpendLens Unit Economics

## Assumptions
- **Average Credex credit deal (ACV)**: $12,000/year (Assuming a 20-person team spending ~$1,000/month on combined AI tools)
- **Gross Margin on Credits**: 30% ($3,600/year profit per customer)
- **Audit-to-email conversion**: 20% (Since the email gate appears at the end, only high-intent or high-savings users will convert)
- **Email-to-consultation**: 10% (Of those who leave their email, 1 in 10 book a call to secure the credits)
- **Consultation-to-purchase (Close Rate)**: 25% (Of those who take a call, 1 in 4 finalize a purchase)

## Required to hit $1M ARR ($1,000,000 / $12,000 ACV = 84 Customers)

At 25% close rate from consultation:
- 84 / 0.25 = **336 consultations needed**

At 10% email-to-consult booking rate:
- 336 / 0.10 = **3,360 leads (emails captured) needed**

At 20% audit-to-email capture rate:
- 3,360 / 0.20 = **16,800 total audits completed needed**

At 50% landing-page-to-audit conversion rate:
- 16,800 / 0.50 = **33,600 unique visitors needed**

## Funnel Velocity & Ramping
To reach $1M ARR in 18 months (~540 days):
- We need to average **31 audits per day** over the 18-month period.
- We need to capture **6 emails per day**.
- We need to book **1 consultation every 1.6 days**.

## CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) Breakdown
Given SpendLens is a free programmatic SEO and viral loop tool, the variable marketing cost per audit is effectively $0. 
However, if we allocate engineering/maintenance time:
- Let's assume a $500/month server, LLM API, and maintenance cost.
- Over 18 months, cost = $9,000.
- $9,000 / 84 customers = **$107 blended CAC per acquired customer**.

With an LTV of $36,000 (assuming 3-year lifespan) and a Gross Margin of $10,800 over that lifespan, an LTV:CAC ratio of >100:1 proves that SpendLens operates as an incredibly efficient, nearly-free lead generation engine for Credex's core credit business.
