## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no login, no trial. The audit is free forever.

**Q: How does SpendLens make money?**
A: We don't, directly. This tool is built by Credex, which sells discounted AI credits. If your audit shows significant savings, we'll tell you about it. If not, we won't mention it.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Your email and company name are never attached to public share URLs. The shareable version shows only tool names and savings numbers.

**Q: How current is the pricing data?**
A: We verify all pricing against official vendor pages weekly. Every data point is timestamped in our PRICING_DATA.md.

**Q: What if I disagree with a recommendation?**
A: Good. The audit is a starting point, not a verdict. Every recommendation includes the reasoning — push back if the context is different than what we assumed.
